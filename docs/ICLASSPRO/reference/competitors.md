# iClassPro Competitor Landscape

Context for evaluating alternatives, understanding market positioning, and identifying feature gaps.

## Direct Competitors

### Jackrabbit Technologies (jackrabbitclass.com)
- **Pricing:** $49-$245/month
- **Strengths:** Industry standard, all-inclusive features, established brand, lower entry price
- **API:** Limited but more available than iClassPro
- **Target:** Gymnastics, dance — strong in both
- **Key Differentiator:** More affordable for smaller operations

### Gymdesk (gymdesk.com)
- **Pricing:** $75-$200/month
- **Strengths:** Modern platform, fixed simple pricing, good analytics, cleaner UI
- **Target:** Newer platform marketing directly against iClassPro
- **Key Differentiator:** Modern design, simpler pricing, better analytics at lower tiers

### Mindbody (mindbodyonline.com)
- **Pricing:** Varies (tends expensive)
- **Strengths:** Enterprise-grade, has API, marketing automation, massive ecosystem, marketplace
- **Weakness:** Very expensive, complex, not gymnastics-specific
- **Key Differentiator:** Actual REST API, marketplace, marketing tools

### Pike13 (pike13.com)
- **Strengths:** Best API/developer platform in the space, enterprise focus, OAuth2 authentication
- **Key Differentiator:** Real developer API — could build anything on top of it
- **Relevance:** If API access is the priority, Pike13 is the benchmark

### ClassManager (classmanager.com)
- **Pricing:** FREE tier available
- **Strengths:** Disruptive free model, dance-focused
- **Weakness:** No API
- **Key Differentiator:** Free disruption model — threatens paid platforms

### The Studio Director (thestudiodirector.com)
- **Strengths:** Dance/gymnastics specialist, costume management feature
- **Key Differentiator:** Dance-industry features like costume tracking, recital management

### Zen Planner (zenplanner.com)
- **Pricing:** $99-$299/month
- **Strengths:** Affordable, martial arts focus, integrated payments
- **Weakness:** Less gymnastics-specific

### WellnessLiving (wellnessliving.com)
- **Strengths:** Broader wellness market, good marketing tools, rewards/loyalty programs
- **Weakness:** Not gymnastics-specific

### Where iClassPro Wins
- **Skill Tracking** — Strongest in the space. Mindbody and Pike13 don't have it.
- **Multi-Sport Support** — Handles gymnastics, dance, cheer, swim, martial arts in one platform.
- **Enrollment Flexibility** — Trial, makeup, waitlist, punch pass, single day — more enrollment types than most competitors.

### Where iClassPro Loses
- **API / Developer Access** — Dead last. Pike13 and Mindbody have real APIs.
- **Marketing Tools** — Mindbody's marketing automation is leagues ahead.
- **Pricing at Scale** — Per-location pricing hurts at 10 gyms. Competitors may offer volume deals.
- **Modern UX** — Gymdesk specifically markets its modern UI against iClassPro's older design.

## Dance Industry Opportunity

### Market Context
- Dance studio management software market growing 7.5% annually
- Market valued at $1.02B, projected to reach $1.7B by 2033
- Dance studios are FURTHER behind gymnastics in technology adoption

### Current Dance Platforms
- The Studio Director (dance-specific: costume management, recital planning)
- DanceStudio-Pro
- ClassManager (free tier disruption)
- Jackrabbit (used in both gymnastics and dance)
- iClassPro (less dance-specific but used)

### Dance-Specific Needs Not Served by iClassPro
- Costume ordering and tracking
- Choreography management
- Recital/performance planning and scheduling
- Competition team management
- Music and routine management
- Rehearsal scheduling (different from regular classes)
- Parent communication for performances (costume requirements, rehearsal schedules)
- Music licensing/management
- Multiple dance styles per student tracking

### Key Differences from Gymnastics
- Seasonal recital/performance cycle (not just ongoing classes)
- Costume logistics are a major operational burden
- Competition teams have different scheduling needs
- Rehearsals are fundamentally different from regular classes

### Opportunity Assessment
The dance industry has similar frustrations to gymnastics but is further behind in technology adoption. A modern, API-first platform designed for both gymnastics AND dance could capture significant market share. The cross-industry opportunity (gymnastics + dance + cheer + swim) is substantial — and it's exactly the multi-sport space iClassPro occupies but doesn't fully serve.
